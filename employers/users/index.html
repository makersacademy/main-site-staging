Recruitment is hard

we do the work for you

We Select

You have to be amazing to study at Makers Academy. We have a tough selection process, that tests for determination and technical aptitude, and accepts less than 10% of applicants. We find exceptional people for you, and then train them.

We Train

Our students spend 10-14 hours a day, 5-7 days a week, learning to be developers. Their code is clean, maintainable and extendable. They're advocates of TDD, pair programming, SOLID and Agile principles. They've learned to build software in teams.

We Match

You get a shortlist of candidates matched (by our engineers) to your needs, based on the in-depth knowledge we have about their commercial background, technical ability, motivations and characteristics - we've spent three months with them.

How will it

Work for you?

1. Post a Job

Complete our job description, which is tailored to the specific needs of the Makers Academy talent pool. We'll use it to create your shortlist.

2. Meet

Once we've done our matching, you can follow your usual process, whether that's a tech test, a pairing interview or drinks at your place.

3. Hire

Most of our candidates get multiple offers and are hired within a few weeks of graduating. Make an attractive offer and secure the top talent.

Who they are

Makers Academy developers are passionate, driven, curious and engaging.

They solve their own problems, are exceptionally fast and effective learners, and understand the business context beyond the code.

The majority of our graduates have been successful in another industry, including law, marketing, banking, design, and many more: that's experience they'll bring to your company.
